Revitalizing Revenue: Data-Driven Business Analysis for Snooker Cafe

This project is a data-driven business and financial analysis of The Snooker Cafe, a snooker-cum-café located in Rohini, Delhi NCR. 

The goal of the project was to understand why the cafe was facing declining sales and profitability and to suggest practical, data-backed strategies for sustainable growth.

Project Overview: The analysis is based on real operational and transactional data collected over three months (April to June 2025). The project focuses on sales trends, cost structures, profitability, menu performance, and customer feedback to identify key business issues and improvement areas.

Data Used
Daily sales data (cash and UPI)
Fixed and variable expense data
Menu-level cost, price, and profit details
Customer feedback survey responses

Methods Used
Sales trend and time-series analysis
Profit margin and operating ratio analysis
Item-wise profitability analysis
Data visualization using charts and tables
Business frameworks such as 7Ps, SWOT, and PESTEL analysis

Key Insights
Sales declined by around 21% over the three-month period
Profit margins dropped consistently month-on-month
A small number of menu items generated a large share of total profit
Several low-performing items increased costs without adding much value
Customer feedback was generally positive, indicating operational issues rather than a lack of demand
